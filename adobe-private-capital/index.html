The Framework

Built on the Customer Experience Delivery Framework.

The CEDF is how IMS has talked about end-to-end customer experience for over a decade. It's deliberately people- and technology-agnostic — the orientation pattern that lets operating partners and CxOs discuss transformation without getting trapped in vendor debates or org-chart politics.

Adobe Private Capital is the first co-branded delivery engine to sit inside the CEDF. It keeps the framework's agnosticism at the strategy layer while giving portfolio companies a concrete, Adobe-powered execution path for the specific work that creates value inside a PE hold: accelerating customer-experience ROI, de-risking inherited stacks, and producing the board-ready evidence of operating leverage.

The engine

The consultant-facing tool behind the compression.

Phase two of the partnership productises the manual run. Discovery inputs against the CEDF, brand tokens scraped from the client URL, template-driven storyboard generation, automated voice-over, one-click preview. Target: ninety minutes from call to client-ready.

Adobe BU and IMS co-invest in the build; Anthropic provides the generation layer; Microsoft stays foundational. The tool ships to APC consultants first, then to named champions inside portfolio companies in a self-service mode.
