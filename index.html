About IMS

Specialists in data, technology,
and customer experience.

Integrated Marketing Solutions is a marketing solutions agency delivering data-driven insight to help clients make better, more confident business decisions.

We work in close partnership with our clients to identify and solve their most complex challenges — combining rigorous analysis with the latest technologies to turn data into actionable insight. We help organisations harness the data they already have to deepen customer understanding, communicate more effectively, and maximise the effectiveness and profitability of their marketing spend.

Our services span end-to-end marketing capability: from marketing strategy and advanced analysis, through to the implementation of Single Customer View and Data Warehouse solutions, and the integration of the full marketing technology stack with enterprise-wide systems.
